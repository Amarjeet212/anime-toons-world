About Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It follows the story of Sung Jin-Woo, a weak hunter who gains the ability to level up in strength and skills after a near-death experience in a dangerous dungeon. As he becomes stronger, he uncovers secrets about the world of hunters and faces powerful foes.
